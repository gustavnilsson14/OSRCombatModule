Combat

GM decides a range of 3~ tiles available for player characters, place minis on mat.

Round lifecycle

  1. Handle battlemat features and combatant conditions
  2. Decide amount of turns
  3. Planning movement and action
  4. Take tactical action

Turn lifecycle

  1. Handle battlemat features and combatant conditions
  2. Possible use of focus points
  3. Move all combatants simultaneously.
  4. Stress equipment
  5. Assists and defends
  6. Ranged attack preparation
  7. Melee and Ranged attacks

Tactical actions

  • Dash
  • Equipment
  • Shrug
  • Catch Breath
  • Cast spell
  • Boost

Actions

  • Melee attack
  • Ranged attack
  • Assist
  • Defend

Stress equipment

  • Weapon durability causes attack to ignore armor
  • Armor durability

Escape

2d8 below distance to closest enemy + (rounds passed * 2)

Critical damage

Add another of the highest die rolled for damage and add to the attack.

Conditions

  • Always add a condition to weapon when aquired
  • One condition can be applied per weapon per round

Conditions can be applied on attack when;

  • Dealing critical damage
  • Stressing the weapon

Wounds and Injury

  • When wounded, max endurance is halved

Random an injury if;

  • Falling unconscious in combat
  • Taking injury out of combat

Gameplay tips!

When taking action;

  • Die rolls mean danger!
  • Be descriptive and specific
  • Use your inventory creatively

When in combat;

  • Death is never sudden
  • Exploit weaknesses
  • Use your inventory creatively
