Rollerthug

Rollerthugs are bandits on rollerblades. They are fast, and surprisingly balanced while having forward momentum.

Attributes

STR 5 DEX 10 WIL 5 EN 35 AV 1 MS 1 DT 3 LVL 1

Tactics

Fights to rob, or preferrably kidnap.

Baseball Bat

DR 1, SR 2 | Smash | Knockback

Weaknesses

Gun Stab Smash Neural

Momentum

The rollerthug gets 2 speed at the end of the turn as long as they move at the start of the turn, and can use this momentum to go past enemies.

If the rollerthug moves towards their target at the start of the turn, they deal another 1d4 damage
