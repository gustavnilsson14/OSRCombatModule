Levels

The car crab gains 5 STR, 1 WIL, 15 EN, 1 AV, and 1 DR to all weapons per level.

Car crabs of different levels have different shells. Level 1 has a fridge, 2 a car, 3 a van, 4 a trailer, 5 a house.
