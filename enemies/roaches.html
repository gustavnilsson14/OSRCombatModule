Wild Roach

Wild roaches are territorial, but herbivore. A skilled wrangler may ride one.

Tactics

Fights to maim, or kill if its brood is threatened. Always charges.

Docile Roach

A docile roach is not aggressive unless harmed, or if its brood is harmed by someone unknown.

Tactics

Tries to scare off, or injure an attacker. Moves as a group, may retreat.
