Movement
Movement on the bridge costs 1 per tile. Combatants not on the bridge cannot usually take any actions unless native. Getting back on the bridge is a tactical action.
Hazard
Combatants can target the bridge to damage it, light damage dealt will set it on fire, also damaging the bridge. After damaged, the bridge collapses at the end of the next round, causing the combat to end. Any combatant on the bridge saves their DEX or suffers a wound.
Feature
There are several clusters of ooze fungi growing on the bridge. Moving onto a tile with fungi requires a DEX save. If unsuccessful, all combatants on the tile take 3d6 ooze damage Toxic Desert Shrubland
