Movement
Movement in the warehouse costs 1 per tile.
Feature
There are stacks of volatile ancient explosives everywhere. Any smash, gun, burst, blast, nuke, or light damage targeting a tile with a stack of explosives has a 25% chance of triggering a detonation (1 on 1d4). When triggered, the explosives detonate at the start of next round. Explosives deal 3d6 blast damage to all combatants, and other explosives in a two tile range in both directions.
