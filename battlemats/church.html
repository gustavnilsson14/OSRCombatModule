Movement
Movement in the dilapidated church costs 1 per tile.
Hazard
The state of the church has changed its otherwise righteous nature into a malicious demeanor. Combatants who use the shrug tactical action suffers 1d10 ooze damage.
Feature
There are ruined columns haphazardly supporting patches of roof in the church. Targeting a column with smash, burst, blast. or nuke damage will knock it over onto the tile opposite to the attacker. When hit by a falling column, a combatant suffers 2d10 smash damage. Walking over a fallen column costs 3 movement. Dilapidated Church
