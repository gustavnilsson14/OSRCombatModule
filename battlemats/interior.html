Movement
Movement costs 2 per tile.
Hazard
Any light damage will cause the targeted tile to catch fire. A tile on fire burns spreads to neighboring tiles at the start of the next round, and after that, expires at the start of the next round. Any combatant ending their turn in fire takes 1d6 light damage.
Feature
There is alot of rubble with can be hunkered down behind as a tactical action, providing immunity to ranged damage while stationary. City Interior
