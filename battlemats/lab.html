Movement
Movement in the bio lab costs 1 per tile.
Hazard
There are large vats of toxins, acids, and other fluids not desirable to get on ones body. Any blast damage in the bio lab will cause fluids to splash on those on the tile of the attacker and target, dealing 3d4 ooze, and nuke damage.
Feature
There is a radiation burst control room which controls strong bursts of radiation in the rest of the lab, for sterilization purposes. When used, it deals 3d10 nuke damage to anything not on the same tile. Can only be used once per combat. Bio Lab
