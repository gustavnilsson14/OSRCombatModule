Movement
Movement on the ruined city streets costs 1 per tile.
Hazard
There are a few manhole covers on the battleground, pressurized by irradiated gas. Stepping on one of the tiles with a cover rerquires a combatant to save DEX or take 3d4 nuke, and smash damage.
Feature
There are old junk cars strewn across the streets, they dont work but can provide cover. When on a tile with a car, a combatant can hunker down as a tactical action, but only if they choose the stand ground movement. A character who is hunkered down cannot be hit by ranged attacks. City Ruins
